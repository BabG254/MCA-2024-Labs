Week 10: Comprehensive Summary

Overview: This week summarizes the analyses conducted over the previous weeks. From rhythmic structure to harmonic progressions, each aspect contributes to the timeless charm of "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star."

Key Insights

  • Rhythm: Balanced and simple, supporting easy singability.
  • Harmonics: Basic yet effective chord progressions in C major.
  • Tempo: A steady 90 BPM ensures accessibility.
  • Frequency Spectrum: Clean, with prominent harmonics.
  • Dynamic Range: Subtle variations maintain interest.

Final Thoughts

This project illustrates how a simple song can serve as an excellent learning tool for musical analysis, offering insights into both theory and performance.
